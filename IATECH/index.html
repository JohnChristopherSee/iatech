What is IATECH?

IATECH Philippines, Corp. is an Automation Technology system integrator company, established with the aim of delivering cost-effective automation and IT solutions for industrial plants, buildings, and utilities.

Our goal is to help our customers achieve Operational Excellence through the effective application of automation technologies that increase manufacturing efficiency and improve plant visibility by providing real-time information—helping management make the right decisions at the right time.

The Core Team

The core team of IATECH is comprised of automation engineers highly-trained and with extensive experience in automation engineering and industrial IT. These engineers have successfully been involved in projects covering a wide variety of applications involving diverse automation technologies in buildings, food & beverage, brewery, tobacco, consumer products, chemical, pharmaceutical, cement, power, utility, and water/waste-water industries.

Therefore, IATECH has the complete expertise and experience to provide total plant solutions—from plant floor to enterprise-wide solutions.

Our core values of innovation, dedication, and excellence will result in solutions that will delight you while meeting, if not exceeding, your expectations.
