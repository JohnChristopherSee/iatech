What is IATECH?

IATECH Philippines, Corp. is an Automation Technology system integrator company, established with the aim of delivering cost-effective automation and IT solutions for industrial plants, buildings, and utilities.

Our goal is to help our customers achieve Operational Excellence through the effective application of automation technologies that increase manufacturing efficiency and improve plant visibility by providing real-time information—helping management make the right decisions at the right time.

The Core Team

The core team of IATECH is comprised of automation engineers highly-trained and with extensive experience in automation engineering and industrial IT. These engineers have successfully been involved in projects covering a wide variety of applications involving diverse automation technologies in buildings, food & beverage, brewery, tobacco, consumer products, chemical, pharmaceutical, cement, power, utility, and water/waste-water industries.

Therefore, IATECH has the complete expertise and experience to provide total plant solutions—from plant floor to enterprise-wide solutions.

Our core values of innovation, dedication, and excellence will result in solutions that will delight you while meeting, if not exceeding, your expectations.

Our Specialty

Providing you with the experience and knowledge necessary to integrate your plants’ automation and IT systems in order to deliver the best data-driven solution to meet your exact business needs.

Harness the power of your plant’s data by integrating your new or existing Data Acquisition Systems, ERP, MRP, and Control Systems.

Working to the highest GAMP standards, IATECH will automate your plants control system information to improve Operating Efficiencies, Reduce Process Downtime and add greater transparency to your manufacturing processes.

SCADA / DCS Enhancement

Your SCADA / DCS applications can also deliver:

  • Batch / Process analysis and reporting
  • Plant performance reports and calculations such as OEE
  • Alarm suppression during maintenance activities
  • Complete user interaction reporting

Overall Equipment Efficiency (OEE)

IATECH helps manufacturers incorporate real-time data into their operations to improve quality, performance, and availability. We collect OEE data from PLC and SCADA systems, calculate it in real-time, and provide dashboards and reports. Our OEE Lite solution can be installed in just two weeks with no machine downtime.

Data Historian

A Data Historian centralizes SCADA, DCS, BMS, PLC, and Data Logger records. IATECH ensures correct installation and offers custom application development so you can unlock value through centralized reporting and data integration across systems.
