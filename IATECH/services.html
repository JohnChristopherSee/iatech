Company Profile

IATECH Solutions, Inc. is a vendor agnostic Industrial Automation Systems Integrator that delivers turnkey solutions from field to enterprise level. Our company is committed to excellence in every solution we deliver to achieve total customer satisfaction.

MES / MIS

IATECH supports the deployment of Manufacturing Execution & Information Systems to connect shop floors to business operations. We design and implement integration with SCADA/DCS systems via third-party historians for seamless data flow.

  • Production Planning / Scheduling
  • Recipe Management (Master & Control)
  • Process Execution (Electronic Batch Reporting)
  • Stock Movements (Goods Issued / Received)
  • Reporting (WIP, Batch, Resources)

Consulting

IATECH consultants provide strategy, design, and system recommendations based on best practices and decades of industry experience. We advise on automation, IT/OT architecture, lean processes, and decision-making improvements.

We provide assessments tailored to your needs, and design future-proof, scalable systems. By delivering real-time information to the right person at the right time, we help you increase ROI through techniques like OEE, ToC, and lean manufacturing.
